Our goal

In a nutshell, Cubert's goal is to efficiently help patients keep track of their medicine dosage in a simple and ubiquitous manner.

RELIABLE

Forgetful of medication? Cubert will always be by your side to remind you.

SAFE

Cubert is bug-less, compact, and the perfect application for those who are currently taking, or may take, prescription drugs.

PRACTICAL

Through rigorous product testing, Cubert's interface has been crafted to be accessible and especially designed for everyday use.

What is Cubert?

Cubert is an android-based app that keeps track of your prescriptions and alerts you to take them. It aims to connect with pharmaceutical companies such as Rite-Aid and Walgreens for automated time recommendations and intends to foster a world without ill-timed drug consumption. In this way, Cubert will eliminate the stress that comes from manually scheduling when to take drugs.
